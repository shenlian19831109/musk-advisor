### 4. Musk's Blind Spots

**5 Major Mistakes:**

| Time | Event | Consequence | Root Cause |
|------|-------|-------------|------------|
| Aug 2018 | Tweet "Considering taking Tesla private at $420" | SEC lawsuit, $20M fine, lost chairman | Emotional decision |
| 2017-2018 | Over-automation | Model 3 production crisis worsened | Engineer thinking vs reality |
| 2022 | Twitter mass layoffs | Brand damage, advertiser exodus | Too aggressive execution |
| 2016 | SolarCity acquisition | Tesla shareholder lawsuit | Related-party transaction |
| 2019 | Tweet "Tesla stock price too high" | Stock dropped 10% that day | Public言论失控 |

**3 Deep Blind Spots:**

```
Blind Spot 1: Over-confidence

Manifestation:
→ Always underestimates completion time (rockets/cars/tunnels)
→ Always overestimates technology maturity (autonomous driving/FSD)

Reason:
→ Physically possible ≠ On time
→ Engineering capability ≠ Management capability

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Blind Spot 2: Ignores "Human" Factors

Manifestation:
→ Thinks everyone should work as hard as him
→ Thinks "equity" solves all motivation problems
→ After layoffs, realized some people were irreplaceable

Reason:
→ Different motivations: some want mission, some want life
→ Information density: his is too high, others can't keep up

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Blind Spot 3: Public Image Too Personalized

Manifestation:
→ Tesla stock often swings with his tweets
→ His personal behavior affects company brand
→ Weed/pot/X controversy

Reason:
→ Personal IP too strong, company fate tied to individual
→ Good: Saves massive advertising costs
→ Bad: Risk highly concentrated
```
